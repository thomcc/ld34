fight or flight

how to play: click to guide the player. hold space to aim. when aiming, clicking shoots a bullet. you are in some sort of a base or space ship and need to find a bunch of keys. there are people who want to stop you from doing this with bullets.

this game was made from scratch by thom chiovoloni in 72 hours as part of ludum dare 34. the theme was "two button controls". the two buttons were the mouse button and the space bar. please play using a recent version of firefox or chrome.
